About Ladybug Bot

Ladybug Bot was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to help individuals and businesses automate their WhatsApp communications to save time and increase productivity.

Our team of developers and communication specialists have created a robust yet easy-to-use solution that integrates seamlessly with WhatsApp to provide powerful automation capabilities.

We believe that technology should work for you, not the other way around. That's why we've designed Ladybug Bot to be intuitive enough for beginners while offering advanced features for power users.

With thousands of happy users across 15+ countries, Ladybug Bot is quickly becoming the go-to solution for WhatsApp automation.

  • Time-based responses

Scheduled Messages

Plan your communications in advance with our intuitive scheduling system. Perfect for birthday greetings, payment reminders, and regular updates.

  • One-time or recurring
  • Timezone-aware
  • Template library

Contact Management

Organize your contacts with custom tags and groups. Create smart lists based on interaction history, location, or other criteria.

  • Custom tags & groups
  • Import/export tools
